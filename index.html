MUSIC & AUDIO

All ambient music and SFX in Naval Strike is 100% procedurally generated using the Web Audio API — no audio files, no recordings, no samples.

Every drum hit, arpeggio, gong, cannon boom, koto pluck, and glitch beep is synthesized live in your browser from raw oscillators, noise buffers, and filters. No equipment. Just code.
